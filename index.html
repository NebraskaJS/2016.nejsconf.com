Venue

The Stanley and Dorothy Truhlsen Lecture Hall is a state-of-the-art theater seating 266 people. Most impressively, it is situated in the old boiler room, part of the original station built in 1899. When the station was torn down in 1929 and the current station completed in 1931, use of the boiler discontinued. In 1997, a $25 million addition and renovation project was completed - this included refurbishment of the original detail and finishes to create an environment to provide proper space for museum experience. via durhammuseum.org
